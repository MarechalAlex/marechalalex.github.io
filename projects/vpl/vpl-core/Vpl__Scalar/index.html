Module Vpl__Scalar

Types for scalar values. Most scalar used in the VPL are rationals. But some applications (e.g. finding a point in the interior of a polyhedron) require the handling of a symbolic error.

  • Rat: zarith rationals
  • Int: zarith integers
  • Symbolic: rationals of the form a + b.delta where delta represents a symbolic error
  • Float: floating points
